# Internationalization rollout (~90% of world, including RTL)

Priority: P2
Stage: Refine
Status: Not started
Track: Translations / i18n

Ship the platform in the native language of most resume-filling users — roughly 90% of countries. Includes right-to-left script support for languages like Arabic and Hebrew. Bulk CMS content (~60k job titles × multiple content types each) is translated automatically via FR's AI-Kit plugin, with human-edit overrides for important strings. Sequenced after the core product is replatformed; depends on the AI-Kit adaptation spike.
